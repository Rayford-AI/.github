Rayford.AI builds **Ray**, the property-level resilience AI for disaster
assessment, claims triage, and recovery intelligence.

Our first workflows, **Ray Assess** and **Ray Claims**, help insurers, adjusters,
local governments, and recovery teams turn pre-event and post-event imagery into
auditable damage evidence.

## Vision

Every property, ready and recoverable.

## Product Direction

- **Ray Assess:** property-level disaster damage evidence.
- **Ray Claims:** claims and inspection triage.
- **Ray Risk:** local hazard history and resilience guidance.
- **Ray Watch:** event monitoring and likely impact detection.
- **Ray Recover:** recovery tracking and assistance guidance.

## Status

Rayford.AI is currently in research commercialization and early customer
discovery. Most repositories are private while the team clarifies product scope,
IP, pilots, and customer workflows.
